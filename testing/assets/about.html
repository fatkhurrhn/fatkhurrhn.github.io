The morning sun warmly greets Depok, streaming through the window of my rented room in Beji-Kukusan. I, M. Fatkhurrohman, or Fathur as I'm known, am getting ready to start my day. As a fifth-semester Information Systems student at Nurul Fikri Integrated Technology College, I've ventured to West Java to pursue my dreams. The scholarship I received has been a huge help on this journey.

I've always been fascinated by technology. My insatiable curiosity drives me to constantly learn and keep up with the latest advancements. In between my busy academic schedule, I channel my creativity by crafting engaging content for social media. Instagram is my favorite platform, with a loyal following of 90,000 users eagerly awaiting my posts. On LinkedIn, I have over 400 followers, and on TikTok, I have over 3,000 followers who are always enthusiastic about my content.

I believe that technology can be a powerful tool for positive change in the world, and I'm determined to keep learning, growing, and contributing to the world of technology, turning my dreams into reality.
